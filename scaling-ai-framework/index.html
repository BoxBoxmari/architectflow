SCALE MODEL

How KPMG scales AI from isolated pilots to embedded ways of working

This framework shows the management system required to increase use-case adoption and measurable value while controlling platform cost.

Prioritised use cases
Sustained adoption

OWNERS AND ACTIONS

Central AI team

  • Define and track value by use case
  • Maintain the enterprise use-case portfolio

Service line leadership

  • Lead implementation by service line
  • Pilot, validate, standardise, then scale

Practitioner community

  • Build practitioner capability
  • Capture quality and adoption feedback
  • Track and improve adoption rates
